Michael Widenius

The creator of the original MySQL


Michael Widenius standing in front of a green backdrop with a MySQL shirt.

Here are some facts about Michael Widenius:

  • In 1985 he founded TCX DataKonsult AB (a Swedish data warehousing company) with Allan Larsson.
  • In 1995 he began writing the first version of the MySQL database with David Axmark. It was released in 1996.
  • He is the co-author of the MySQL Reference Manual, published by O'Reilly in June 2002.
  • In 2003 he was awarded the Finnish Software Entrepreneur of The Year prize.
  • Selling MySQL to Sun Microsystems, Widenius earned about €16.6 million in capital gains in 2008 (€16.8 million total income), making the top 10 of highest earners in Finland that year.
